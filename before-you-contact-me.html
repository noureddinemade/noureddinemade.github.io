I like being upfront & honest about what I’m looking for. So my ideal role is being a hands on principle designer / head of design for a small / early stage startup getting paid between 165k — 200k AUD + Super.

Role I’m looking for

Principle Designer / Head of Design

Type of company

Small / Early stage startup

Salary range

$165k — $200k AUD + Super

Ownership & autonomy

If you decide to work with me, you will need to trust my judgement, my professional opinion and my experience. So if you ask me to solve a particular problem I need to have to be given the creative freedom (within reasonable bounderies) to be able to solve that problem and not be micromanaged.

Remote & flexible working

I’ve freelanced for most of my career, I’m well organised, I don’t miss deadlines and I always deliver. If your company’s thinking is that you are paying for me to be in an office every day from 9-5 rather than paying me to deliver then this absolutely will not work.

Culture & diversity

Are the majority of your managers, c-suite and board members white? Do you hire diversly but don’t accomodate for that diversity? Does your company’s social culture revolve heavily around getting drunk? At this stage of my career I don’t want to be your company’s token and I don’t want to work with garbage people, so if the answer to any of those questions is a yes, then please stay away.

Interviewing & hiring

I’ve spent a lot of time carefully putting this portfolio together specifically for people wanting to work with me. It clearly highlights what, how and why. I’d love to have a chat and run you through my work and process but if your hiring process requires that I perform a design task then I’m afraid it won’t work out.
