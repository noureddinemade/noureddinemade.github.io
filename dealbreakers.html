So, these are my dealbreakers. If the place you’re hiring for has one, pretty please don’t contact me. I’m usually a very nice person but if you’re from a company that has any of these dealbreakers and you contact me anyway, I will assume that you are a piece of shit and will respond in kind.

  1. I don’t trust the judgement of an all white management team. So if the majority of your managers, c suite and board members are white, then I 100% don’t want to be any where near your trash company.

  2. If you hire diversely but your space is not accomodating to us and our diversity, then obviously that means we are just an item on your cool PR checklist and that’s super gross.

  3. Being the only non white person in a team is mentally exhausting. I’ve experienced it, a lot. And honestly, I just don’t have the energy to be your company’s resident token.

  4. If your team building events revolve heavily around getting drunk because your CEO thinks this builds stronger teams, then that is not on brand for me and also, your CEO sucks.

  5. Does your CEO descirbe living in their parents guest house with absolute financial security and daddy helping them get their product off the ground as struggling? Then no thank you.

  6. If you don’t offer flexible hours or remote working then no thanks. I don’t want to be in a office all day so that Chad (your CPO I’m assuming) can look over my shoulder all day.

  7. The vast majority of your employees don’t like working outside of their normal working hours, so don’t encourage this bullshit. Let them live their lives.

  8. You think these dealbreakers make me a difficult person to work with when really, I’m a fucking treat to work with and you’re genuine trash.
