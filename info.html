Who am I?

Hi there, I’m Noureddine. I’ve spent the last fifteen years doing design, so presumably, I know what I’m doing. And if that’s not enough and you’re still wondering if I know what design is, of course I do. It’s two single colour shapes on a white background surrounded with lots of white space.

Creative problem solving is at the heart of everything I do. Over the years, I finally figured out that my role is simple, I solve problems. For example, if someone has a squeaky door I would grease the hinges first then, I would cover the door with glitter, because design.

When I’m not designing, you can find me hanging out in Naarm (Melbourne) with my human lady friend and our cat Porchester. I like antique markets, rewatching dumb action movies, eating cone shaped ice cream and trying to make a board game.

What can I do?

End-to-end design, interface design, user experience design, branding, webflow, front-end development, product strategy and come up with lists.
